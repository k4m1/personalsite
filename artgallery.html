Context and naming conventions

Below you will see that my collection of art work follows a strict and simple naming convention. "untitled" and then the next number in the sequence that encompases all of my art. It is a purposeful desiicion to keep the interpretation of my art work free of pre-dispot and supotsition. It also serves the purpose of keeping all of my work easily referencable in a chronilogical order. As someone who tends to always be creating it helps give me needed perspective and, an easy frame of referance.
