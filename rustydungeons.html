Rusty Dungeons

A procedualy generated "rogue-like" game build using rust.

Features and Challenges

Outlined below are some of the features that I am most proud of having implemented. Alongside the challenges of implementing these features and what I learned along the way.

Procedural Generation

Everything that the player explores, slays or loots in Rusty Dungeons is a part of the procedural generation content cycle. All of the rooms and connecting hallways are dynamically generated and connected. The placements of monsters and loot are also dynamic in this way.
